Adobong Manok

Adobo is considered the unofficial national dish of the Philippines. It was a marinating and cooking meat with vinegar and salt that was later influenced by Spanish colonization, introducing soy sauce and other spices.

Ingredients:


1 kg chicken or pork
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup vinegar
1 head garlic (crushed)
2 bay leaves
1 tsp whole peppercorns
1 tbsp sugar (optional)
1-2 cup water
Cooking oil

Procedure:


1. Marinate meat in soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes
2. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, add marinated meat and cook until lightly browned
3. Add vinegar, bay leaves, sugar, and water
4. Let it boil without stirring
5. Simmer for 30 to 40 minutes until meat is tender and sauce is reduced
