Igado (pork)

Igado is a popular Ilocano dish composed of pork meat and liver stewed with vinegar, soy sauce, and vegetables. It's a reflection of Spanish influence name from "higado," Spanish for "liver".

Ingredients:


12 kg pork tenderloin (sliced into strips)
14 kg pork liver (sliced into strips)
14 cup soy sauce
14 cup vinegar
1 small carrot (julienned)
1 red bell pepper (sliced)
12 cup green peas
1 onion (chopped)
4 cloves garlic (minced)
Bay leaves, salt, and pepper

Procedure:


1. Saut√© garlic and onion
2. Add pork tenderloin, cook until browned
3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar; simmer without stirring
4. Add liver, carrots, bell peppers, and peas
5. Simmer until liver and vegetables are cooked. Adjust seasoning

Carbs 7 g
