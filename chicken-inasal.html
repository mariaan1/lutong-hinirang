Chicken Inasal

Chicken Inasal is a beloved Filipino dish, particularly popular in the Visayas region. It's characterized by its distinctive marinade and grilling method, resulting in juicy, flavorful chicken with a vibrant orange hue.

Ingredients:


Marinade: The marinade is the heart of Chicken Inasal.
Vinegar (often coconut or cane vinegar)
Citrus juice (calamansi, lime, or lemon)
Lemongrass
Garlic
Ginger
Brown sugar
Annatto oil (achiote oil)this gives the chicken its characteristic orange color
Spices (salt, pepper, chili powder)
Atsuete oil (the remaining annatto oil from cooking)
A mixture of soy sauce, calamansi juice, vinegar, and chili peppers
Sinamak (a spiced vinegar)

Procedure:


1. Marinate chicken pieces in a mixture of calamansi juice, vinegar, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, brown sugar, and annatto oil for at least 1 hour or overnight.
2. Prepare the basting sauce by mixing annatto oil, margarine or butter, and a bit of leftover marinade.
3. Preheat a grill over medium heat and oil the grates lightly.
4. Grill the chicken while basting regularly with the annatto butter mixture until fully cooked and slightly charred.
5. Served hot with garlic rice, dipping sauce (soy sauce, vinegar, and chili), and optionally, sinamak(spiced vinegar).
Nutrition Facts per 1 cup serving
Nutrients:
Calories 213 kcal
Protein 20 g
Fat 12 g
Carbs 5 g
Sodium 297 mg
