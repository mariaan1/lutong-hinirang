Bicol Express

Originating from the Bicol region, famous for its love of spicy foods, Bicol Express is a creamy, and spicy dish made with coconut milk and chilies.

Ingredients:


12 kg pork belly (sliced)
2 cups coconut milk
1 cup coconut cream
5 to 10 green chilies (sliced)
1 onion (chopped)
4 cloves garlic (minced)
2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:


1. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrantr
2. Add pork and cook until fat renders
3. Stir in shrimp paste, coconut milk, and chilies
4. Simmer until pork is tender and sauce thickens
5. Add coconut cream and adjust seasoning
