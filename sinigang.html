Sinigang (pork)

Sinigang is a traditional Filipino sour soup, often made with tamarind. It's loved for its sour flavor and its good with meats and seafood.

Ingredients:


1 kg pork belly or ribs (or shrimpfish)
1 pack tamarind soup base or fresh tamarind
1 onion (quartered)
2 tomatoes (quartered)
1 radish (sliced)
1 bundle kangkong (water spinach)
1 eggplant (sliced)
2 to 3 long green chilies
Fish sauce, to taste
6 cups water

Procedure:


1. Boil pork in water until tender
2. Add onion and tomatoes; simmer
3. Add tamarind base and vegetables (radish, eggplant, chilies)
4. Simmer until vegetables are cooked
5. Add kangkong last; season with fish sauce
Nutrition Facts per bowl
Nutrients:
Calories 270 kcal
Protein 20g
Fat 15g
Carbs 8g
Sodium 750mg
