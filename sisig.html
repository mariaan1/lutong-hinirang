Sisig

Sisig is a Kapampangan dish made traditionally from pigs head parts and liver, seasoned and served sizzling. It is now a famous Filipino pulutan and main dish.

Ingredients:


12 kg pork cheeks or belly
14 cup liver (optional)
1 onion (minced)
2 green chilies (sliced)
1 egg (optional, for topping)
2 tbsp mayonnaise
2 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp calamansi or lemon juice
Salt and pepper

Procedure:


1. Boil pork until tender, then grill until slightly charred
2. Chop pork into small cubes
3. Saut√© onion, add pork and liver
4. Season with soy sauce, calamansi juice, salt, and pepper
5. Add mayonnaise for creaminess
6. Serve on a sizzling plate; top with raw egg if desired
Nutrition Facts per 1 cup serving
Nutrients:
Calories 400 kcal
Protein 22 g
Fat 30 g
Carbs 4 g
Sodium 820 mg
